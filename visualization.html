FAFSA Application Trends and Institutional Factors

This project explores our dataset (cleaned.csv) with multiple charts. We can switch among quarters (Q1..Q5) for selected charts, and we have an aggregated line chart and stacked bar chart for Q1..Q5.

This analysis examines quarterly enrollment trends (Q1â€“Q5) across 1,271 U.S. educational institutions in different states. The dataset compares dependent and independent student enrollment patterns at public, private, and proprietary schools, identified by OPE ID, name, location, and type. The cleaned and standardized data enables deeper investigation of seasonal enrollment impacts, financial aid policy effects on student dependency ratios, and regional education disparities. These insights support policymaking, academic research, and institutional planning by revealing comparative enrollment trends and enabling predictive modeling. The preprocessing work established a strong foundation for robust statistical analysis of these educational patterns.
